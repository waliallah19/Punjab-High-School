Welcome to Punjab High School


We are a leading educational institution committed to providing high-quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our mission is to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures intellectual curiosity and prepares students to become responsible and productive members of society.

We offer a wide range of academic programs, including STEM, humanities, social sciences, and arts, that are designed to cater to the unique needs and interests of each student. Our experienced and dedicated faculty members are committed to helping students achieve their full potential.

Our History


Punjab High School was founded in 1992 by Mian Asif Ali , a visionary educator who wanted to create a school that offered a rigorous and comprehensive education to students from all walks of life. Since then, the school has grown to become one of the most respected and well-regarded educational institutions in the country.

Over the years, we have continued to expand our academic programs and facilities to meet the evolving needs of our students. We are proud of our rich history and remain committed to providing our students with the best possible education.
